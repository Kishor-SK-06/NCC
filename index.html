NCC Hierarchy

National Cadet Corps

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a prestigious youth development movement in India that instills discipline, leadership, and a sense of national service among students. It operates under the Ministry of Defence and provides military training to school and college students across the country.

Gujarat Directorate

In Gujarat, the NCC is administered by the Gujarat Directorate, which oversees all NCC activities across the state. This directorate is responsible for coordinating training programs, camps, and ceremonial events.

Baroda Group

Within the Gujarat Directorate, the Baroda Group plays a vital role in managing NCC operations in and around Vadodara. It supervises several units and organizes social service initiatives and adventure activities.

2 GUJ Naval Unit

One of the key units under the Baroda Group is the 2 Gujarat Naval Unit NCC, based in Vadodara. This unit specializes in naval training and provides cadets with hands-on experience in seamanship and sailing.

Narmada College

Narmada College, located in Bharuch, is one of the prominent institutions affiliated with the NCC under the Baroda Group. The college actively encourages its students to enroll in NCC.
